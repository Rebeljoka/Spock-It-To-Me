Game Types
  • Best of 3: First to win 2 out of 3 rounds wins the game.
  • Best of 5: First to win 3 out of 5 rounds wins the game.
  • Endless: Play as many rounds as you like, no limit.
Difficulty Levels
  • Easy: Computer picks moves randomly.
  • Medium: Computer sometimes counters your last move.
  • Hard: Computer often counters your most frequent move.
